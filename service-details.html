HIFU 7D Lifting Facial — The Non-Surgical Facelift

HIFU (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound) 7D is the gold standard non-surgical lifting treatment, delivering concentrated ultrasound energy at multiple tissue depths simultaneously. It stimulates the body’s natural collagen production, resulting in tighter, firmer, more lifted skin — without any incisions, injections, or recovery time.

At Serenova, we use the latest-generation 7D HIFU device, which reaches 7 different depths from 1.5mm to 16mm — targeting everything from superficial skin layers to the deep SMAS (muscle) layer for dramatic, long-lasting results.

Zero downtime & non-invasive

Full face in 60 minutes

Who is it for? HIFU 7D is ideal for adults aged 30–65 experiencing mild to moderate skin laxity on the face, neck, chin, and décolletage. It is particularly effective for those wanting to lift jowls, tighten the neck, reduce nasolabial folds, and improve overall skin texture and firmness.

  • Lifts and tightens sagging skin on face and neck
  • Reduces fine lines and wrinkles significantly
  • Defines jawline and chin contour
  • Tightens the décolletage and chest area
  • Improves skin texture and pore appearance
  • Results continue improving for 3–6 months post-treatment

Step 1: Consultation & Mapping — Your specialist conducts a thorough skin analysis, photographs treatment areas, and creates a personalized treatment map based on your unique anatomy and goals.

Step 2: Preparation — A cleansing gel is applied to treatment areas. No numbing cream is typically required, though it can be provided for sensitive clients.

Step 3: HIFU Application — The device delivers precise ultrasound energy to 7 skin depths. Most clients feel a warm, tingling sensation. The full face treatment takes 60–90 minutes.

Step 4: Soothing & Review — A calming serum and SPF are applied. Your specialist reviews results and provides your personalized aftercare plan.

HIFU has minimal downtime. Most clients return to normal activities immediately. To maximize your results and protect your skin:

  • Apply SPF 50+ sunscreen every morning for 2 weeks
  • Avoid direct sun exposure for 48 hours post-treatment
  • Do not use retinol or active acids for 5 days
  • Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily to support collagen synthesis
  • Mild redness or slight swelling is normal and resolves within 24 hours
  • Follow-up visit recommended at 4–6 weeks to assess progress

Is HIFU painful? Most clients describe it as a warm, tingling sensation. Sensitivity varies by area — bony areas like the chin may feel more intense. We offer numbing options for sensitive clients.

How soon will I see results? Some lift is visible immediately after treatment. Peak results develop over 3–6 months as new collagen is built, and typically last 12–18 months.

How many sessions do I need? Most clients achieve their goal in 1–2 sessions. Maintenance sessions once a year are recommended to sustain results.

Who should avoid HIFU? Pregnant women, clients with active skin infections, metal implants in the face, or certain medical conditions should consult their physician first.
