Our Story

Dedicated to Your Radiance

Founded in 2000, Serenova Wellness began as a small boutique spa with a single mission: to make every person feel beautifully confident. Over 25 years, we have grown into one of Metro Manila’s most trusted premium beauty and wellness clinics.

We combine ancient Asian wellness traditions with the latest evidence-based aesthetic technology. Every treatment is personally designed, every ingredient is thoughtfully chosen, and every client is treated like family.
